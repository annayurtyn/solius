03

Team Allocation

25%
Auto-Staking

Of the total 35% team allocation, exactly 25% is automatically staked from day one. The minimum lock-up period is 24 months, with a staking horizon of 2 – 10 years at the team's discretion. This portion fully participates in staking rewards and the flywheel model.

10%
Milestone-Based Unlock

The remaining 10% unlocks exclusively upon reaching milestones defined in the whitepaper on May 15, 2026. The minimum requirement is a valuation of $300M. Until that threshold is crossed, the team receives no tokens from this pool.

05

Flywheel Model

25%
Staker Distribution
A quarter of platform revenue flows directly to staked token holders — monthly dividends in $SOLIUS and USDC. The longer you stake, the higher your share.
75%
Buyback & Burn
Three quarters of revenue are used to buy back tokens from the open market and burn them permanently. Fewer tokens in circulation means higher value per token — a built-in deflationary mechanism.
How the Flywheel Works
Platform grows → generates revenue
Revenue → 25% stakers + 75% burn
Burn reduces supply → $SOLIUS price rises
Higher price → more users & liquidity
More users → higher platform revenue
Exact token supply figures, staking multipliers and milestone conditions will be published in the official whitepaper no later than May 15, 2026. All transactions run fully on-chain on the Solana network.
